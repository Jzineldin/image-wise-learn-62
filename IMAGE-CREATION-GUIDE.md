## üé® WEEK 1: "The Beta Founder Response"

### Option 1: Inbox Screenshot (Recommended)

**What to show:**
- Your email inbox with multiple messages
- Subject lines visible (blur sender names)
- Highlight 3-5 positive messages

**How to create:**
1. Open your email
2. Take screenshot (Cmd+Shift+4 on Mac, Win+Shift+S on Windows)
3. Open in Preview/Paint
4. Blur out names/emails (use blur tool)
5. Add text overlay: "127 Beta Founders in 48 Hours"

**Tools:** Just screenshot + basic editing

---

## üé® QUICK CANVA TUTORIAL

### Step-by-Step for Beginners:

**1. Create Account:**
- Go to canva.com
- Sign up with email (free)
- No credit card needed

**2. Choose Template:**
- Click "Create a design"
- Select "LinkedIn Post" (1200x627px)
- Or "Twitter Post" (1024x512px)
- Search for template type (e.g., "infographic")

**3. Edit Template:**
- Click on text to edit
- Click on images to replace
- Drag elements to move
- Use color picker for brand colors

**4. Add Your Content:**
- Text: Click "Text" ‚Üí "Add a heading"
- Images: Click "Uploads" ‚Üí Upload your photos
- Icons: Click "Elements" ‚Üí Search for icons
- Shapes: Click "Elements" ‚Üí Search for shapes

**5. Download:**
- Click "Share" ‚Üí "Download"
- Choose PNG (best quality)
- Click "Download"

**Time:** 10-15 minutes per image

## üé® TALE FORGE BRAND COLORS

Use these colors in Canva for consistency:

**Primary Colors:**
- **Amber:** #FFA500 (main brand color)
- **Gold:** #FFD700 (accent)
- **Purple:** #8B5CF6 (secondary)

**Background Colors:**
- **Dark:** #1A1A2E (dark mode)
- **Light:** #F8F9FA (light mode)

**Text Colors:**
- **White:** #FFFFFF (on dark backgrounds)
- **Dark:** #1A1A2E (on light backgrounds)

**How to use in Canva:**
1. Click on element
2. Click color picker
3. Click "+"
4. Enter hex code (e.g., #FFA500)
5. Save to brand colors

## üí° PRO TIPS

### Make Images Stand Out:
1. **Use contrast** - Dark text on light background (or vice versa)
2. **Keep it simple** - One main message per image
3. **Use white space** - Don't cram everything in
4. **Be consistent** - Use same fonts/colors across posts
5. **Add your logo** - Small logo in corner for branding

### Save Time:
1. **Create templates** - Save your designs in Canva
2. **Batch create** - Make 4 images in one session
3. **Reuse layouts** - Change text/images, keep layout
4. **Use brand kit** - Save colors/fonts in Canva
