## Barriers to Entry

### For New Entrants

**High Barriers:**
1. **AI Integration Complexity** - Requires expertise in prompt engineering, API integration, error handling
2. **Content Safety** - AI safety for children is non-trivial (filtering, moderation)
3. **COPPA Compliance** - Legal requirements for children's apps (parental consent, data privacy)
4. **Multimedia Pipeline** - Coordinating text, image, and audio generation is complex
5. **User Experience** - Balancing simplicity (for children) with power (for creation)

**Medium Barriers:**
6. **Database Design** - Complex schema for stories, segments, characters, credits
7. **Credit System** - Fair pricing and cost management
8. **Multilingual Support** - Requires language-specific AI models and testing

**Low Barriers:**
9. **Frontend Development** - React/TypeScript is standard
10. **Hosting** - Managed services (Supabase, Vercel) are accessible

**Overall Assessment:** **High barriers to entry** - requires 6-12 months and $100K-$500K to build a competitive product.
